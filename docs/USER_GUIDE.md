## Getting Started

### Accessing the Dashboard

- Open your web browser and navigate to: `http://localhost:3000` (or the URL provided by your administrator).

### Logging In

1. Click on the **Login** button.
2. Enter your **Username** and **Password**.
3. Click **Submit**.

**Note**: If you do not have an account, please contact your system administrator.

---

## Navigating the Dashboard

After logging in, you will be presented with the main dashboard containing various sections.

### Sales Overview

- Provides a summary of key sales metrics, such as total sales, number of orders, and average order value.

### Sales Trends

- Visualizes sales data over time using the **Sales Trend Chart**.
- Allows you to identify patterns and trends in your sales data.

### Sales Forecast

- Predicts future sales using AI models.
- Input future dates to generate predictions.

### Customer Segmentation

- Groups customers into segments based on purchasing behavior.
- Helps you tailor marketing strategies for different customer groups.

### Anomaly Detection

- Alerts you to unusual sales activities.
- Anomalies are highlighted on the dashboard for quick attention.

---

## Managing Sales Orders

### Creating an Order

1. Navigate to the **Orders** section.
2. Click on **Create New Order**.
3. Fill in the **Customer Name** and **Order Items**.
4. Click **Submit** to save the order.

### Viewing Orders

- Use the **Sales Data Table** to view existing orders.
- Utilize the **Pagination** controls to navigate through pages.

### Deleting Orders

1. In the **Sales Data Table**, locate the order you wish to delete.
2. Click on the **Delete** button associated with that order.
3. Confirm the deletion when prompted.

---

## Settings and Preferences

### Language Selection

- Click on the **Language** dropdown in the header.
- Select your preferred language from the list.

### Profile Management

- Access your profile by clicking on your username in the header.
- Update your personal information and change your password as needed.

---

## Troubleshooting

- **Cannot Log In**: Ensure your credentials are correct. Contact the administrator if you continue to experience issues.
- **Data Not Loading**: Check your internet connection. If the problem persists, try refreshing the page.
- **Error Messages**: If you encounter any error messages, note the details and contact support.
